The Blue Bear Risies

The Blue Bear Rises follows Davey Fishey (65) and Geordie (81) - both lifelong fans of their beloved club GLASGOW RANGERS and both committed to following their team - recently relegated to the bottom tier of Scottish football but clawing their way back Year on Year to their rightful place in the Scottish Premier League. A moving tail told from very emotional fans giving us a full season (2013/2014) insight into the dedication, passion and love still felt by many in Scotland and around the world - a club that can still attract 38,000+ fans to every game.
